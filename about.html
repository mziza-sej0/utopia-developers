Our Story

Founded by a small group of engineers and designers, Utopia Developers grew from freelance roots into a focused agency delivering web products for startups and SMBs.

  • 2018: Company founded — first projects in marketing sites and prototypes.
  • 2020: Expanded to full-stack applications and APIs.
  • 2022: Focus on performance, accessibility and long-term maintenance.
  • 2024: Remote-first team delivering across timezones.
